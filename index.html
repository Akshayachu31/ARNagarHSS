About Our School

A.R.NAGAR HSS CHENDAPURAYA was established in 1924 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in VENGARA block of MALAPPURAM district of Kerala. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Malayalam is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Private building. It has got 61 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 16 boys toilet and it is functional. and 26 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 5000 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 40 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab. The school is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal.
